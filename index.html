About

School of submission was founded in late 2021 by Abbas, a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt practitioner who attained his skill under Mauricio Gomes and Roger Gracie, Gracie Academy, West London. This has given him the abiltity to help the community in and around Alperton to participate in bjj and provide the youth a avenue to pursue a better, fitter lifestyle.

We are located within Markaz Us-Sunnah mosque in Alperton (near Wembley). Adult classes are on Wednesday evenings and Friday evenings (usually around 8pm). All students must attend wearing appropriate clothing (eg. bjj uniform for bjj gi classes) and be in a clean state (to avoid spreading of disease). If you would like to know more about the venue please visit Markaz Us-Sunnah. Any other questions, feel free to send us a message using the form below.
